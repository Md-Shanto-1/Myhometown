About Place to Visit Culture

Comilla is a city in eastern Bangladesh, known for its rich history, educational institutions, and vibrant culture.
It is one of the oldest cities in the region, with ancient archaeological sites and historical landmarks.

  • Mainamati Ruins – ancient Buddhist archaeological site
  • Comilla War Cemetery – honoring soldiers from WWII
  • Shalban Vihara – ancient Buddhist monastery
  • Dharmasagar – large historic pond and park area

Comilla is famous for its traditional sweets like Rasmalai, local music, and folk dance.
The city celebrates various festivals with great enthusiasm.
